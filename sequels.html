Beginner's Guide

After Disney bought LucasFilms, in 2015, a new trilogy starts. Rey, a scavenger, sees herself in the middle of a millennia long battle, the light against the dark, the Resistance (a military force fighting for the New Republic) against the First Order (a terrorist militia trying to re-establish the glory and Order of the old Empire).

Episode VII - The Force Awakens

Rey joins Finn (a Rogue stormtrooper) to find the Resistance and helps to find Luke Skywalker, who has vanished. On that journey, the couple and the Resistance finds out yet other platform capable of destroying planets. Han Solo dies by his own son’s hand, Kylo Ren who fights Rey when she awakens her Force powers.


Episode VIII – The Last Jedi

Rey tries to get Luke Skywalker to train her. The Resistance is caught between a rock and a hard place when trying to flee from the First Order fleet. Useless stuff happens in Canto Bite. Luke force projects himself to face Kylo Ren. The Resistance is saved.
This movie can very controversial. It split the fan-base in haft because of the director’s story choices that, at times, felt disingenuous and not respecting the Star Wars lore.

Episode IX - The Rise of Skywalker

J.J. Abrams comes back to write and direct this one to ‘fix’ the last director’s direction to Star Wars, because of that the plot suffers with retconning and convolutedness. The story comes back to story beats explored on episode VII, it is found out that Ray was Palpatine’s granddaughter. Kylo Ren gets his redemption and joins Rey to defeat the Emperor and end the Sith.
