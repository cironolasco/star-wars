Beginner's Guide
Started in 1977, the original trilogy set off one of the world’s most well-known fictional franchises.
Star Wars became a pop culture phenomenon, changed the way movies were made, and started a merchandising revolution.
Everyboy wanted to wield a lightsaber and use the Force.

STAR WARS - Episode IV - A New Hope

When struggling to live his old life behind, Luke Skywalker is forced to move on from Tatooine with Ben Kenobi to larger and harsher reality ending with the destruction of the Empire’s Death Star, a space station capable of destroying planets. On the Star Wars timeline, much happened before it, but this was the first Star Wars story ever told. It is known for being the perfect example of the Hero’s Journey.
