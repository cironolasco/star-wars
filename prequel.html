Beginner's Guide

These movies told three stories, Anakin’s journey as a Jedi and his fall to the Dark Side, Palpatine’s rise to power, the last days of the Republic and the ascension of the First Galactic Empire. The movies have mixed feelings from the fans as they are not very well directed and the narrative can by convoluted at times, but expand the Star Wars universe tenfold.

Episode I - The Phantom Menace

Young Anakin is taken under Qui-Gon Jin’s wing as a padawan. The Sith reveal themselves after 1000 years of dormancy. Qui-Gon dies by the hand of Darth Maul, who is cut in half by Obi-Wan Kenobi.


Episode II – Attack of the Clones

The Jedi start to feel that the galaxy is moving to a dark path. War is on the horizon. The Separatists start to gain influence in the Outer Rim of the Galaxy. Young Anakin falls in love with Padmé Amidala. Obi-Wan finds out a Clone Army ordered by the deceased Jedi Master Sifo Dyas. The Clone Wars begin and Anakin and Padmé get secretly married.

Episode III - Revenge of the Sith

Anakin sees the future and Padmé dies during child-birth. On his path to try and stop this dark future to happen, he gets closer and closer to the Supreme Chancellor Palpatine, who pushes Anakin to the Dark Side. The Jedi find out that Palpatine is a Sith Lord and try to overthrow him. Anakin helps Palpatine who executes Order 66 and all clones turn against their former friends and leaders, the Jedi. Obi-Wan and Anakin face off. Anakin loses and become Darth Vader A new era begins in the galaxy, the First Galactic Empire.
